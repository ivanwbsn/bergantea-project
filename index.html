About Us

  • Story behind BERGANTEA

    BERGANTEA Industry, inspired by the Indonesian word *berganti* meaning "change," is a premium tea brand dedicated to shifting daily habits from coffee to a healthier alternative. With our tagline, *"Waktunya untuk BERGANTEA"* ("Time for a Change"), we’re bringing the richness of Indonesian tea to the world, one cup at a time.
